================
IRC Chat on #esp
================

There is a `#esp <irc://irc.freenode.net/esp>`_ chat channel on `IRC
<http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Internet_Relay_Chat>`_ for live collaboration.
Just enter your nickname below and join in!

you can also access it with these details:
